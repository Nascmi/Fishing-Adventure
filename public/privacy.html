Privacy Policy

Fishing Adventure

Effective July 21, 2026

Fishing Adventure is developed and published by Nathan Miller. This policy explains how Fishing Adventure handles information when you use the game.

Information Fishing Adventure does not collect

Fishing Adventure does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, precise location, contacts, photographs, advertising identifier, or gameplay analytics. The game contains no advertising or third-party advertising SDKs.

Game progress stored on your device

Your catches, coins, equipment, cabins, settings, achievements, and other game progress are stored locally on your device. Nathan Miller does not receive or maintain this game-save data on a remote server.

Google Play purchases

Optional purchases are processed by Google Play. Fishing Adventure requests product availability, localized prices, purchase status, and ownership information needed to complete and restore purchases. The game does not receive or store your payment-card or bank-account details. Google handles payment information according to the Google Privacy Policy.

Sharing catches and cabins

Fishing Adventure can create catch and cabin images locally on your device. An image leaves the game only when you deliberately use the Share feature and choose a destination through your device's share interface. The receiving app or service handles that image under its own privacy policy.

Device features and permissions

The game may use device vibration for optional haptic feedback and internet access to communicate with Google Play for product and purchase services. It does not request access to precise location, contacts, microphone, camera, or broad device storage.

Data sharing and sale

Nathan Miller does not sell personal information. Fishing Adventure does not share personal information for advertising, marketing, tracking, or data-broker purposes. Purchase processing is performed by Google Play as described above.

Data retention and deletion

Locally stored game progress remains on your device until you use Reset progress, clear Fishing Adventure's app data in Android settings, or uninstall the app. Because Fishing Adventure does not currently provide accounts or remotely store game saves, there is no remote game account or cloud save for Nathan Miller to delete. Google may retain purchase records under its own policies and legal obligations.

Children's privacy

Fishing Adventure is a general-audience game and is not designed to collect personal information from children. Because the game does not provide accounts, advertising, chat, or analytics, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Fishing Adventure minimizes data handling by keeping game progress on the device and relying on Google Play for payment processing. No method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed completely secure, but the game does not intentionally transmit local save data to Nathan Miller.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when Fishing Adventure's features or legal requirements change. Material changes will be reflected on this page by updating the effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact Nathan Miller at nathansmiller1982@gmail.com.
