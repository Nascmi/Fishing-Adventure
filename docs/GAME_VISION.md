# Fishing Adventure Vision

## Mission

Fishing Adventure is a peaceful place players choose to visit. It recreates the feeling of standing beside a quiet lake, casting a line, and wondering what might bite next.

The player should enjoy the moment-to-moment experience as much as the progression.

## Pillars

### Relaxation

The game should reduce stress. It must not use advertisements, forced waiting, expiring streaks, fear of missing out, or punishment for taking a break.

### Discovery

Every trip should hold the possibility of finding a new species, an exceptional specimen, or a memorable location.

### Progression

Progress should feel earned and understandable. Better equipment should create new opportunities without making earlier catches worthless.

### Collection

The collection is a long-term record of the player's journey, not a checklist that demands obsessive grinding.

### Exploration

Each future location should have a distinct atmosphere, fish population, and reason to return.

## Product Identity

Fishing Adventure is not a casino, gacha game, competitive esport, social platform, notification machine, or idle game without meaningful interaction.

Optional purchases may eventually support development, but spending must never be required for basic enjoyment or gameplay power.

## Success

The game succeeds when players:

- understand it quickly;
- enjoy fishing for either five minutes or an hour;
- make meaningful progress at their own pace; and
- close the game feeling a little calmer than when they opened it.

When evaluating a feature, ask whether it makes the game more peaceful, rewarding, beautiful, or understandable. If it does none of those things, leave it out.
