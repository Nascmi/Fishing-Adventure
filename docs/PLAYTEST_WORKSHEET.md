Record a species tally only when someone is deliberately testing a target lure. Small samples can be interesting, but they are not enough on their own to prove that a probability needs changing.

## Economy and Progression

- Did anyone feel stuck earning toward a wanted purchase?
- Did any purchase feel trivial, overpriced, or especially satisfying?
- Were charter relocation fees understood before accepting?
- Did permanent boats and lures feel worth owning?
- Did players naturally switch waters, areas, rods, or lures? Why?

## Reproducible Issues

For a bug, note the device, location, selected area, rod, lure, what was tapped, what happened, and whether it happened again. Add a screenshot when the problem is visual.

## Balance Review Rule

Preserve the current balance until repeated sessions show a pattern. Favor observed player behavior and several comparable samples over one unusually lucky or unlucky run.
