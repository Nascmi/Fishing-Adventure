# Roadmap

The roadmap records approved direction, not a promise of fixed dates. Polish and playtesting may change the order.

## Phase 1 — Playable Foundation

- Fishing loop at Willow Pond
- Inventory and fish selling
- Rod shop and equipment progression
- Fish collection
- Player statistics
- Local save system
- Mobile-first responsive interface

## Phase 1 Polish

- Playtest timing, catch rates, values, and rod progression
- Improve accessibility, including bite timing options
- Add production-quality icons and fish artwork
- Bundle fonts and essential assets for reliable offline play
- Add calm sound, music, and optional haptics
- Strengthen save recovery and migration behavior

## Phase 2 — A Wider World

- Additional fishing locations
- Location-specific fish populations
- Weather that changes atmosphere without creating pressure
- Optional, non-expiring fishing goals
- Meaningful achievements

## Phase 3 — Deeper Personalization

- Boats and lures
- Aquarium
- Cabin and cosmetic customization
- Seasonal fish that do not rely on fear of missing out

## Version 1.0

A polished, accessible, offline-friendly fishing game ready for public Android and iOS release.

Unapproved concepts remain in `FUTURE_IDEAS.md` until deliberately promoted here.
