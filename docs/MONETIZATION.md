# Monetization

Monetization is not part of the current roadmap. If introduced later, it must support the game without changing the quality or pace of free play.

## Never Allowed

- Advertisements
- Energy systems or paid wait-skips
- Loot boxes or randomized purchases
- Paid gameplay power
- Artificial inconvenience sold back as a solution
- Expiring offers designed to create pressure

## Potentially Acceptable

- Cosmetic rods and boats
- Cabin decorations
- Clearly described cosmetic bundles
- A straightforward supporter pack

Prices, purchase restoration, parental considerations, platform requirements, and offline behavior must be designed deliberately before commerce is implemented.
